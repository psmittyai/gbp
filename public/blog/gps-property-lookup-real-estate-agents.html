GPS Property Lookup: Pull a Full Report at the Curb — No Typing

One tap. Your phone detects where you are, pulls the address, and delivers a live AVM, tax records, and neighborhood scores before you walk to the door.

Every agent knows the moment. You pull up to a house — maybe a showing, maybe a comp drive, maybe a buyer just texted you an address. You want the data right now. Instead, you're typing an address on a phone keyboard while sitting in a car.

RealtyAssistAI's GPS Property Lookup eliminates that friction entirely.

How It Works

  1. Open the app on your phone and tap Property Report from the Tools tab.
  2. Tap "📍 Use My Location" — one button, green, top of the form. That's it.
  3. Allow location once — your phone's GPS pinpoints your coordinates in seconds.
  4. Address auto-fills — street, city, and state populate automatically via reverse geocoding.
  5. Live report streams in — AVM estimate, comparable sales, tax records, school district, neighborhood health scores. Everything you need before you knock.
Real example: Agent pulls up to 342 Baldwin Rd in Birmingham, MI. Taps Use My Location. In 8 seconds: 3BR/3BA Colonial, 2007 build, AVM $1.335M, comparable range $854K–$1.82M, Oakland County taxes. Walks to the door knowing the property better than the listing agent expects.

Military Proximity Auto-Detection

The GPS feature does one more thing automatically: it checks whether you're near a military installation. If you're within 30 miles of one of 60 US military bases, the app surfaces a VA buyer context alert.

Agent near Camp Pendleton? The app flags: "🎖️ 8mi from Camp Pendleton — VA buyer tips available." One tap opens the full Military/VA Buyer strategy tool, pre-filled with the base proximity. No setup, no searching.

This is how you work a military market efficiently.

Why This Matters More Than It Sounds

Zillow uses GPS to show you nearby listings. That's a consumer feature. What agents need is different — they need the data layer underneath a specific property, delivered at the moment they're standing next to it.

No other agent AI tool does this. Most of them are chat interfaces you sit at a desk and type into. RealtyAssistAI is built for how agents actually work — in the car, at the curb, in between showings, on a phone.

Privacy Note

Location is requested in the moment, for that lookup only. Nothing is stored or tracked. The GPS prompt uses your browser's native permission system — you can allow once, always, or never. The feature works just as well if you type the address manually.
