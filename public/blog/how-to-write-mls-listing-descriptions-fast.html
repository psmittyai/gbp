What makes a good listing description in 2026?

The fundamentals haven't changed — but AI gets them right every time:

✓ Lead with the lifestyle, not just the specs
✓ Highlight 2-3 standout features specifically
✓ Include neighborhood context buyers care about
✓ End with urgency without being pushy
✓ Stay within MLS character limits
✓ Avoid fair housing red flags

Pro tip: use your own voice

After the AI generates the copy, read it out loud. If it doesn't sound like you, change a few words. The goal is speed — not replacing your judgment. Use AI to get to 90% in 10 seconds, then spend 30 seconds making it yours.
