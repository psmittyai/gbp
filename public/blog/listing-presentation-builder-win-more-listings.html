What Actually Matters in a Listing Presentation

A winning presentation doesn't reinvent the wheel. It hits five core pillars that sellers actually care about:

1. Property Overview. Not just "nice place." What makes this specific property standout? Unique features, recent improvements, buyer appeal points—the stuff that matters to the right buyer.

2. Pricing Rationale. Why this price? Comparable sales, market trends, absorption rate, days-on-market data. Sellers want to know you didn't just guess.

3. Marketing Plan. How are you going to get this in front of buyers? Photos, staging tips, social strategy, MLS strategy, open house plan. Concrete steps, not vague promises.

4. Why You. Why does this seller need *you* specifically? Track record, market knowledge, your unique approach. This is trust-building ground.

5. Net Sheet. Show them the money. What do they actually walk away with after costs? Transparency kills objections fast.

That's it. Five things. But getting them all right—gathering the right data, organizing it, presenting it clearly—takes time.
