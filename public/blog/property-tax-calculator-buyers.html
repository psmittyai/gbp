How to Answer "What Are the Property Taxes?" Without Guessing

April 18, 2026 · 3 min read

It's the most common question buyers ask at showings — and the one most agents handle worst. "What are the property taxes on this place?" And the typical answer is some version of "I'll find out and get back to you."

Why agents fumble this question

Property taxes are genuinely complicated. They vary by county, school district, municipality, and sometimes even neighborhood. The assessed value doesn't always match the sale price. Mill rates change every year. Most agents know their core market but go blank the second a client asks about a county they don't work in every day.
